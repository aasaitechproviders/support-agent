How it works

From knowledge to live agent in three steps

No developer, no complex setup. Just your content and a few clicks.

01

Upload your knowledge

Add PDFs, Word docs, images, plain text, or live URLs. Aasai extracts and indexes everything automatically using Document AI.

02

Configure your agent

Set your agent's name, brand colors, logo, welcome message, and personality. Pick from 5 ready-made chat themes.

03

Publish and share

One click to go live. Get a shareable URL instantly. Anyone can chat — no login required on their end.
