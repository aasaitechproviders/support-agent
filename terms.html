02 · What Aasai Does

An AI agent platform powered by your knowledge

Aasai is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that allows users to create AI-powered chat agents trained on their own knowledge bases. You can upload documents, paste text, or provide URLs, and Aasai converts that content into a conversational AI agent accessible via a shareable link.

The AI responses are generated using Google's Gemini language models. Aasai acts as the orchestration layer — we manage the knowledge base, user interface, branding, and delivery. We do not build or own the underlying language model.

📂
Knowledge Storage
Your uploaded content is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas. It is only used to answer questions from your published agents.
🤖
AI Responses
Responses are generated by Gemini 2.0 Flash. We send your knowledge and the question; Gemini returns an answer.
💬
Conversations
Visitor conversations are stored and made accessible to you via the dashboard. They are never shared or sold.
🎨
Branding & Delivery
We host the chat interface with your branding, colors, and logo. The experience is entirely yours to customise.
03 · Data We Collect

Only what's necessary to run the service

We collect the minimum data needed to operate Aasai. Here is a complete list of what we store:

Account Information (Users)

  • Name and email address from your Google account (via Google OAuth)
  • Profile photo (from Google, or one you upload)
  • Organisation name and phone number (optional, if you provide them)
  • Plan type and token usage counts
  • Account creation date and last login timestamp

Agent Content

  • Knowledge base content you upload: text, extracted text from files, content scraped from URLs
  • Agent configuration: name, system prompt, brand color, theme, welcome message, logo URL

Conversation Data

  • Questions asked by visitors to your published agents
  • AI-generated answers
  • Visitor IP address (used for grouping conversations by session)
  • A session ID and visitor ID generated per browser session (stored only in the visitor's localStorage)
  • Timestamp of each message
  • Token count per message (for billing and quota tracking)

We do not collect: credit card numbers (handled by payment processors), passwords (we use Google OAuth only), location data beyond IP address, device fingerprints, browsing history, or any biometric data.

04 · No AI Training — Our Core Commitment

Your data will never train any AI model

🛡️
Hard commitment, not a preference: None of the data you upload, create, or generate on Aasai — including knowledge bases, conversation logs, uploaded files, or system prompts — is ever used to train, fine-tune, evaluate, or improve any AI model. This applies to Aasai's systems and to any third-party model providers we work with.

Specifically, we commit that:

  • Your knowledge base content (PDFs, text, URLs) is used only to answer questions from your published agents. It is never used as training data.
  • Visitor conversations (questions and AI responses) are stored for your dashboard analytics only. They are not fed back into any AI training pipeline.
  • File uploads (PDFs, images, documents) are processed for text extraction and then referenced for answering questions. They are never used to train models.
  • Google Gemini API usage: We use Google's Gemini API under terms that prohibit Google from using our API inputs to train their models. API data is not used for training per Google's API terms of service.
  • We will never sell your data to any third party, including AI research organisations, data brokers, or advertising networks.

If we ever update our data practices in a way that could affect this commitment, we will notify you via email at least 30 days in advance and provide a clear mechanism to delete your data before any such change takes effect. You will always have the option to delete your account and all associated data.

05 · How We Use Your Data

Only to operate and improve Aasai

We use the data we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service operation: Running your AI agents, storing your knowledge base, displaying your dashboard, and processing queries.
  • Billing and quota: Tracking token usage against your plan limit. We need to know how many tokens you've consumed to enforce plan limits and calculate charges.
  • Analytics for you: Populating your dashboard with query counts, session counts, and token usage charts. This data is yours — you can see it, manage it, and delete it.
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymised platform statistics (total queries, total tokens across all users) are used to understand platform-level usage and improve reliability. This never includes your specific content.
  • Communication: Sending you important service-related emails (plan limits approaching, account updates). We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Security: Detecting abuse, unauthorized access, or violations of these Terms.
06 · Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their commercial purposes. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Google Gemini API: Your knowledge base and visitor questions are sent to the Gemini API to generate answers. This is the core function of the service. Google processes this data under their API terms, which prohibit using it for training.
  • Google OAuth: We receive your name, email, and profile photo from Google when you sign in. We do not share anything back with Google beyond the standard OAuth flow.
  • MongoDB Atlas: Your data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database). MongoDB has access to your data for infrastructure purposes but is contractually prohibited from accessing it for any other reason.
  • Cloudflare: Our frontend is served via Cloudflare for performance and security. Cloudflare processes request metadata (IP addresses, headers) as part of their CDN service.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Aasai, our users, or others.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics platforms. There are no ads on Aasai, and we do not track you across the web.

07 · Security

Reasonable measures to protect your data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication is handled via JWT tokens with 30-day expiry and Google OAuth — we never store passwords
  • Database access is restricted to authorised application servers only
  • API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables and never exposed in code
  • File uploads are processed in memory and never written to disk unencrypted

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at [email protected]. We will investigate and respond promptly.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
